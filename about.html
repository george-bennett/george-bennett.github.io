Heya, I'm George!

I'm currently working as a Survey Data Officer for the Insititute of Social an Economic Research, based at the University of Essex, where I blend my passion for data with my technical expertise. With a strong foundation in CSS, HTML, JavaScript, SQL, Stata, Python, and R. I thrive at the intersection of technology and design, and I'm particularly fascinated by survey data, web development, and the intricacies of UX, UXD, and UXR.

I have an academic background in both humanities and science, holding an BA in Modern History along with a MSc in Computer Science, from the University of Essex and the University of East Anglia respectively. During my MSc I developed a keen interested in the concepts of UX / HCI within a website and applications enviroment.

Beyond work, I love capturing moments through photography which you can see here on its dedicated page, alongside my instagram, diving into immersive video games, and experimenting with video production. I'm always excited by new technologies and love talking about them near endlessly!.
